Wellstar Bright Secondary School – History & Overview 🏫 Welcome to Wellstar Bright Secondary School – Excellence, Discipline, and Holistic Education! 🏫

Brief History of Wellstar Bright Secondary School

  • Founded in 1995 and registered by the Ministry of Education and Sports.
  • Initially operated in Kireka and Bweyogerere (Wakiso District) before relocating to Mukono along Mukono–Kayunga Road.
  • Established to provide both O-Level and A-Level education, offering arts and science subjects.
  • Grown into a mixed day and boarding school focusing on academic excellence, discipline, and moral values.

Type of School

  • Private secondary school
  • Mixed (boys and girls)
  • Day and boarding
  • Offers both O-Level and A-Level (arts & sciences)

🏛️ Foundation Body

  • Founded by private individuals (non-government)
  • Registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports (Uganda)

📍 Location

  • Located in Mukono District, Uganda
  • Specifically along Mukono–Kayunga Road (Kabembe area)

🎓 Motto

🎯 Core Values / Aims
